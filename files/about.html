About Us

We open doors so kids can find their sound.

The Backstage Pass Project is a youth-led nonprofit empowering underprivileged young people through music, creativity, and live experience.

Our Mission

Empowering youth through music & creativity

We seek to empower underprivileged youth by giving them access to creativity, music, instruments, and therapy. We believe that music has the power to inspire, heal, and unite communities from all walks of life.

Our Vision & Community

A world where music reaches every young person

We envision music as a universal tool for personal growth, emotional wellness, and community connection — building a creative ecosystem that inspires long-term confidence, belonging, and opportunity.

What We Do

Programs that put youth center stage.

We expose underprivileged youth to the arts through community programs, workshops, and concerts — creating spaces to explore creativity and heal through sound.

Music Therapy Sessions

Guided programs that use sound and rhythm for emotional healing and self-expression.

Instrument Donation Drives

Collecting and distributing gently used instruments to kids who cannot afford them.

Workshops & Creative Labs

Hands-on experiences with local musicians, producers, and artists.

Community Concerts

Bringing youth, schools, and performers together to share the stage and the story.

Our Story

A small movement growing into something bigger.

What started in Cleveland is growing into a connected network of musicians, schools, and communities united by the transformative power of music. We've seen the spark in kids who hit their first chord or hear live music for the first time.

The Backstage Pass Project is powered by a growing network of mentors who believe in the same mission: that every young person deserves a backstage pass.
